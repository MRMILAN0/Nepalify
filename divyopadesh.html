The 52 Core Teachings of King Prithvi Narayan Shah (1723тАУ1775). Foundation of the modern Nepali State.

Ascending the throne of Gorkha in 1743, he launched a strategic campaign to consolidate the fragmented Himalayan principalities into a single sovereign nation. His unification preserved Nepal's independence during an era of colonial expansion.
